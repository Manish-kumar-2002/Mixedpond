Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the Readiness Proof Index (RPI)?

RPI is a comprehensive assessment tool that evaluates a company's readiness for investment or revenue generation across key indicators.

What information does RPI provide?

RPI offers detailed information about companies, categorizing them based on four distinct factors: Leadership, Market Readiness, Organizational Readiness, and Culture. This information can be downloaded in PDF format, aiding both investors and companies.

Who can benefit from using RPI?

Empowering investors and Preparing companies
